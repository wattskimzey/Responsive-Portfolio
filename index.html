About Me

I was born in the back of a pumpkin cart, traversing the lowlands of southern Arizona. Troubling though the heat was, we were able to survive on pumpkin juice until we were able to set up shop in a small town near the southern border.


Pumpkin sales never peaked as much as they had for the family back East where the melting pot of larger cities clamored insatiably for pumpkins. The Southwesterners were just not interested in "fancy gourds".


Needless to say our small pumpkin based income withered on the vine. Father and the oldest boys took whatever odd jobs they could find, no matter if low paying or back-breaking work.


So, I'm over pumpkins. The End.
